About

Cloudripper Labs is an independent research effort focused on AI interpretability and understanding how language models represent and process information.

The name comes from Cloudripper, a peak in the Sierra Nevada—a nod to both cloud computing and the aspiration to see clearly from above.

Current Focus

Replicating and extending introspection research on open-source models, using TransformerLens to probe model internals.
